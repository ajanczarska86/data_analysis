## Key Insights
- Users spend over **65% of their day sedentary**, with very limited time in high activity zones.  
- Most calories are burned during **low-intensity activities**, suggesting consistent light movement throughout the day.  
- Step activity peaks between **5â€“8 PM**, indicating after-work engagement.  
- Weekend activity levels are slightly higher than weekdays.

---

## Visualizations
- Bar charts: average calories by intensity level  
- Line charts: activity trends by hour and weekday  
- Pie chart: distribution of activity intensity throughout the day  

---

## Recommendations
- Encourage users to **increase moderate activity** by gamifying movement goals.  
- Use notifications to remind users to move after long sedentary periods.  
- Target marketing campaigns around **evening fitness habits**.
